About Me

Who I Am

I'm Charles Appiah-Manu — a Software Engineer, Full-Stack Developer & Bioinformatician currently pursuing my M.Sc. in Computer Science (Bioinformatics) at Georgia State University, Atlanta.

I build across the entire stack: crafting pixel-perfect front-end interfaces with React & modern CSS, architecting back-end systems with Python Flask & Supabase, and applying computational biology tools (BLAST, MEGA, IGV) to genomics challenges. My work spans health tech, learning analytics, and responsive web applications.

Beyond code I bring a strong UI/UX sensibility — I research users, wireframe in Figma, and test until the experience feels effortless. Whether it's a clinical data pipeline or an anime streaming platform, I care deeply about quality at every layer of the stack.
